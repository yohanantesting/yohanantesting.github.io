About Arise

"To deliver affordable and accessible patient-centered care, inspiring you to live well - mind, body, and soul."

At Arise , our brand story is rooted in a powerful message of renewal and vigilance. The Hebrew word for almond, "shaked," literally translates to "watch," and it serves as our guiding light. Like the almond tree's blossoms, which are the first to emerge at the end of winter, we believe in the power of new beginnings and the unwavering spirit of hope and diligence.

As a devout Christian and a dedicated physician, our founder's passion is to witness people rise and become the best versions of themselves. We invite you to embark on a journey of self-discovery and well-being with us as we watch over your health and help you reach your full potential.
