About Us

Preamble

Moved by the conviction and the desire to promote, improve and protect human life and health through productive relief and developmental interventions while upholding human dignity coupled with other prudent societal issue needing the attention of stakeholders necessitated the establishment of “De Precious Rubies Hub” which shall be the official name of the Organization.

The organization shall be non-political, voluntary and Non-Profit base with no desire of undertaking any business with the aim of profit.

Mission

Mission Statement

Unleashing Potential, Sparking Transformation - At Precious Rubies Hub, we believe that every individual has unique gifts and talents waiting to be discovered. Our mission is to help unearth these gems and empower individuals to live their best lives.

Our mission is to:

Develop and implement effective solutions to address pressing emotional challenges especially among women and young people.

Collaborate with diverse stakeholders to strengthen family systems, in the communities.

Support and empower individuals, especially women and young people to be useful to themselves and their communities thereby improving the genera well-being of the society.

Advance research, innovation, and education to drive progress in healthcare and health outcomes.

Advocate for policies and practices that prioritize human life, dignity, and well-being.

Through our work, we aim to:

Reduce suffering and morbidity.

Improve health outcomes and quality of life.

Enhance healthcare access, affordability, and equity.

Foster a culture of empathy, inclusivity, and respect for human rights.

Continuously learn, adapt, and improve our efforts to address the evolving needs and challenges of humanity.

Vision

Vision Statement

Empowering a world where every individual can thrive and reach their full potential, free from suffering and inequity. We strive to:

Support and empower individuals, communities, and other professionals to make informed decisions and take action.

Foster inclusive and equitable access to quality healthcare, education, and resources.

Protect and preserve the integrity of individuals and communities and the resources available to them.

Continuously learn, adapt, and improve our efforts to address the evolving needs and challenges of humanity.

This vision statement highlights the organization's commitment to:

Improving human life and health through innovation and technology.

Promoting equity and access to healthcare and resources.

Empowering individuals and communities to take control of their health and well-being.
