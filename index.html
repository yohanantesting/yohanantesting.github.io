Leadership

The problem of human kind is leadership without morals, skills and competence.

Character: We are developing leaders with divine understanding of the principles of God: Integrity, Faithfulness and Trustworthiness - to establish the purpose of God on planet earth.

Our mission is to lift up the standard of church leadership with moral excellence among Pastors and potential leaders to impact our world.

Our Purpose

Empowerment

To empower men and women to play a leadership role effectively within the family, ministry, govenment and the community at large. To make life more meaningful for human kind.

Ignition

To ignite leadership potential in leaders to become all that they are destined to be by God.

Increase

To increase the revelation and intellectual capacities of leaders.

Impartation

To impart wisdom and knowledge for the development of virtues, ethics and character.

Encouragement

To encourage growth in leadership, personal accountability, discipline and relationship.
