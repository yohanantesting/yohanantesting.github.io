About Firm Foundation LLC

Board Certified Physician
High Standard of Care

Firm Foundation Health and Wellness Center, established in 2024 by Dr. Agyenim Amoako and Patience Agbeviadey, aspires to offer comprehensive medical and mental health services in Kent County, Delaware. Their mission is to provide exceptional, holistic care in a centralized location, ensuring seamless coordination and continuity of patient care. The clinic's approach revolves around personalized care plans designed to cater to the unique needs of each patient, fostering overall well-being and facilitating optimal health outcomes.

Our holistic approach addresses interconnected health facets, resulting in more effective and enduring outcomes.
